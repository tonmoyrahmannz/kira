# Leftovers

## Cuisine
Mixed

## Servings
3

## Cook time
10 minutes

## Pregnancy safety notes
- Reheat leftovers until piping hot all the way through.
- Avoid reheating cooked rice more than once.

## Ingredients
- Leftover cooked meals from earlier in the week
- Optional quick sides: cucumber sticks, fruit, yogurt

## Step-by-step cooking instructions
1. Reheat leftovers to steaming hot.
2. Serve with a fresh side if needed.
3. Cool and refrigerate any remaining food promptly.

## Kid friendly serving notes
- Offer familiar portions and simple sides.
- Cut food into small, safe pieces.

## Leftover reuse suggestions
- Use oldest cooked meals first.
- Freeze extra portions before quality drops.
