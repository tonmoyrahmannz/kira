## Planning Rules
- Do not prioritise buying these items unless:
  - a specific variation is required,
  - required quantity is unusually high, or
  - a recipe depends heavily on one item and current stock may be insufficient.
- Treat this file as baseline assumptions, not a real-time stock count.
- Use `inventory/Pantry_Inventory.md` for meaningful current stock and use-soon items.
