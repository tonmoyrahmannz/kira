## Maintenance Schedule

### Weekly
- Check weeds
- Remove weeds
- Inspect pests
- Harvest mature leaves
- Remove damaged foliage

### Fortnightly
- Apply liquid seaweed fertiliser or vegetable fertiliser

### Monthly
- Add compost
- Add sheep pellets
- Review plant health
- Review seasonal planting opportunities

## Watering Rules

### Summer
If no significant rain for 3–4 days:
- Check soil moisture
- Water deeply

### Winter
If no significant rain for 7+ days:
- Check soil moisture manually
- Water only if top 5cm of soil is dry
