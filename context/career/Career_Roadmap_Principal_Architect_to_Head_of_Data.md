# Career Roadmap: Principal Data Architect / Head of Data (2026–2031)

## Vision

Build a career path from Data Architect to Principal Data Architect and ultimately Head of Data, while establishing a respected independent advisory practice through Whiro Analytics.

The objective is not to become an expert in every technology, but to become a trusted advisor who can evaluate, select, govern, and lead enterprise data platforms and teams.

---

# Long-Term Career Target

### 3–5 Year Goals

Potential target roles:

- Principal Data Architect
- Enterprise Data Architect
- Head of Data Platforms
- Head of Data & Analytics
- Head of Data & AI
- Fractional Head of Data
- Independent Data Strategy Consultant

Target positioning:

> "A strategic Data Architect who helps organisations modernise data platforms, improve governance, enable analytics and AI, and deliver measurable business outcomes."
