# Bank Account Structure

## Canonical Active Bank Accounts

The household uses a joint-account bucket system with separate personal accounts.

Current recommended active structure:

```text
1. Tonmoy Personal
2. Esha Personal
3. Joint Everyday / Operations
4. Joint Bills
5. Joint Savings / Emergency Fund
6. Airbnb / Rental Income
7. Daycare
8. Property & Rates
```

This structure is intentionally designed to balance visibility, discipline, and simplicity.

---

## Account Purposes

### 1. Tonmoy Personal

Purpose:

```text
Tonmoy personal spending / no-questions personal allowance
```

Use for:

- Personal discretionary spending
- Individual purchases
- Small personal treats
- Items not requiring household-level review

Principle:

This protects autonomy and reduces friction in household spending.

---

### 2. Esha Personal

Purpose:

```text
Esha personal spending / no-questions personal allowance
```

Use for:

- Esha personal discretionary spending
- Individual purchases
- Small personal treats
- Items not requiring household-level review

Principle:

This is especially important while Esha is on maternity leave or working part-time, so personal spending remains fair and independent.

### 3. Joint Everyday / Operations

Purpose:

```text
Day-to-day family operating account
```

Use for:

- Food
- Fuel
- Eating out
- Entertainment
- Small household purchases
- General everyday family spending

Notes:

- Grocery and food spending should come from this account.
- This account is for variable living costs, not major fixed bills.

### 4. Joint Bills

Purpose:

```text
Core fixed household commitments
```

Use for:
